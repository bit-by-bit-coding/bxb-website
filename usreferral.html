Uncommon Sense Seminar Application

To apply for Uncommon Sense, we require that you first get a teacher nomination. Please ask the teacher you know best to nominate you. However, if you are homeschooled, or have other extenuating circumstances, please contact us.

Once you are nominated, you may apply by May 30th. Once you apply, we will review your application; if you seem promising, we will request an interview by June 1st. Decisions will be released by June 30th.

Nomination & Application Deadline: May 30th.
Interviews: June 1 - June 7.
Final Decisions: June 30th.
