Hello World Volunteer Details

What: Hello World, a summer camp that teaches middle school girls the basics of web development. Find out more at https://bitbybitcoding.org.

Who: You! We’re looking for volunteers who know (or are willing to learn) the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Programming experience is appreciated but NOT required -- we provide free training!

When: 2:00-5:00pm Aug. 3-7 and Aug. 10-14. You don’t have to come every day; just days that work for you! Expect to do at least one hour of volunteer training beforehand, or more if you don’t know how to code.

Sign up: https://bit.ly/bxbvolunteering. If you know anyone interested in attending the camp, please direct them to https://bit.ly/bxbinterestform!

Benefits: Gain up to 25+ volunteer hours (or choose to do less), join a community of programmers, and empower girls to code. We’ll teach you invaluable coding and leadership skills for free, and you can gain loads of volunteer hours or a position on your resume while you’re at it.

Contact us at {{site.contact.email}} for more info. We’ll keep you posted about Coronavirus developments :)
