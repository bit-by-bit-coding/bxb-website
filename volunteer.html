Hello World Volunteer

: teach HTML, CSS, and/or JavaScript at our summer program for female and non-binary middle school students

Director and Assistant Director

Lead the Hello World summer program as you inspire the next generation of coders.

Who: Someone with coding and leadership skills who is dedicated to our mission and improving our programs.

When: The time commitment for the Director and Assistant Director of Hello World is around 3-5 hours a week during the school year and 10 hour a week during the summer, increasing to 15 hours a week during the weeks of the program.

Benefits: Gain invaluable leadership and project management experience, collaborate with others, sharpen your communication skills, and plan the future of the program.

More information coming soon!

BxB Central Volunteer

: assist with the behind-the-scenes work in our marketing, fundraising, software engineer, and website teams

Team Lead

We are currently looking for team leads for our software engineer team, website team, marketing team, and fundraising team.

Who: Someone with key organizational, communication, and leadership skills. Each team also comes with their own responsibilities and expected expectations.

When: The time commitment for the team lead positions is around 6-8 hours a week.

Benefits: Join a collaborative and supportive team, sharpen your leadership skills, and manage a team of dedicated volunteers to achieve Bit by Bit's goals!

Standard Volunteer

Volunteer within the marketing, fundraising, software engineer, or website team!

Who: Anybody who wants to contribute to our mission and support our programs. With a wide range of responsibilities within BxB Central, we will have a task for you!

When: Unlike the programs, there is no set time and we are much more flexible with meetings and work.

Benefits: Join a community of supportive, dedicated volunteers while gaining invaluable experience in whichever team you decide to join and collaborate with others on long-term projects!

Form and Function Volunteer

: help with our online after-school program that teaches middle school students the core of web development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript)

Standard Volunteer

Form and Function, an online afterschool program that teaches middle schoolers how to code an interactive website in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Bit by bit, students will learn to code!

Who: You! We’re looking for volunteers who know the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A little programming experience is required but we are happy to teach more! We provide free training for coding instructors. Volunteers teach curriculum and have fun!

When: There are three sessions for Form and Function: Mondays from 4:30 to 5:30pm PT, Wednesdays from 4:30 to 5:30pm PT, and Fridays from 4:30 to 5:30pm PT. Volunteer for all three days or only one. Either way we would love to have you! Form and Function will run from the week of the 3rd of October through the week of the 22nd of May. Volunteers will have mandatory training sessions weekly.

Benefits: Gain loads of volunteer hours, join a group of individuals and introduce middle schoolers to the power of code. We’ll teach you coding, communication, and instructional skills so you can gain invaluable experience in these fields!
