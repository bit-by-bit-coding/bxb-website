Hello World Volunteer Details

What: Hello World, a summer camp that teaches middle school girls the basics of web development.

Who: You! We’re looking for volunteers who know (or are willing to learn) the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Programming experience is appreciated but NOT required! We provide free training for coding instructors, and we have plenty of non-coding-related opportunities in graphic design, social media/brand management, outreach, finance, law, and more.

When: 2:00-4:00pm PST on Aug. 3-7 and Aug. 10-14. You don’t have to come every day; just days that work for you!

Benefits: Gain loads of volunteer hours, join a community of programmers (and friends), and empower girls to code. We’ll teach you invaluable coding and leadership skills for free, and you can gain a position on your resume while you’re at it.
