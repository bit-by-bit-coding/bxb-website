Covid-19 Policy

Due to Covid-19, we are not certain whether or not our program will be in-person or online. If we have to move the program online, we will not be able to provide laptops, but we will provide around 2 hours of fun, interactive instruction on the same dates! Any payment accepted at that time will be fully refunded minus a credit card processing fee. We’ll keep you posted on developments.

Register soon to reserve your spot! Sign up here and we’ll notify you of any updates.

  • Social Distancing: If the camp is in-person, we may limit the number of participants to ensure that we are within state and local limits and can maintain 6 feet of distance between campers.
  • Food: We will likely switch to providing only pre-packaged food or will take other precautions to minimize the number of people in contact with the food, such as by having a designated volunteer wearing adequate protection dispense any snacks.
  • Check-In: Campers will likely need to go through additional screening, such as temperature checks and/or other Covid-19 related tests.
  • Additional Precautions: All campers must wear face-coverings.
