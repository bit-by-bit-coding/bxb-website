Adopting A Hedgehog

An article by MK

Have you ever wanted a cute, exotic, rare pet? A pet that that fits all of those catagories is a hedgehog!

Hedgehog are cute and adorable interesting little pets. If you want to buy a hedgehog, you better start saving up, they're not free!

A good quality hedgehog is usually priced from 100-350 dollars. Try looking online to find a LEGAL breeder. DO NOT buy a hedgehog off of Amazon or Ebay.

Those are ILLEGAL breeders, do not buy off of them. Some materials you may need as a hedgehog owner are a cage, meal worms, cat food, food dish,

water dispenser, litter box, hedgehog bedding, igloo, playpen, and hedgehog medicine. That is all you need to know to get a hedgehog!
