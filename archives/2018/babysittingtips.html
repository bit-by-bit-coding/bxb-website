Kids activities

  1. for young babysitters (or old)
An easy and fun activity is making home made popsicles.
You can do that by getting popsicle molds juice and/or mashed up fruit. Its easy and its delicious


Another fun idea is making a sack race. This is a fun race and gets them moving. you can get them for not too much and use them over and over.

Another fun idea to do with them is this kit that helps you make your own book. Its some thing you can do with everybody and its so fun

Another very fun idea is making a fort. You can do this with chairs cloth and anything else to hold the cloth up its fun and easy and should bring smiles to the kids faces. But of course you should ask their parents if its ok and also clean up the mess after.

If you are babysitting girls you could pull out a tea set and have a tea party. All you need is a tea set and tea.If you are doing a tea party with little girls you do not have to use tea. You can use milk or almond milk with vanilla and sugar. This is not the only recipe but it is delicious!

Another idea is playing fun games that everyone can participate. Some good games are suspend, mimic,and quick cups. All of these games are games that everyone can play. But any game is good.

Another idea for girls is taking any left over beads and string and you can make bracelets. And if you do not have and beads you can get some for super cheap.




Another idea is playing restaurant. You can do this a million ways. One way is getting cardboard and cutting it into food. Then color it and get the play menus, (or paper) its fun, its easy,and its crafty.



Get ready to pull out a chair as the curtains open for a puppet show!!! This is a fun thing all you need is cardboard and puppets.

Don't worry if you do not have puppets cause you can make sock puppets. All you need is a sock,googly eyes,glue, and the thing that you make the mouth with optional. Another fun and crafty thing is making a puzzle. Its inexpensive and it is very fun.




This part of the website is show you tips that can make sure the parents want you to come back.

1 One thing that you should definitely ask is that if there are any allergies.

Another really good question to ask is what to do if they are bad.

This sounds like a mean question but you don't want them to act up.
