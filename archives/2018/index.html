Hello World Websites!

These websites were made by girls going into 6th through 9th grade in just one week as part of a summer program called Hello World! 27 girls participated in the program, but some opted not to make their sites public. They are all wonderful and definitely worth a look. The program will likely run again in the the summer of 2019, and a less formal web-development program may be offered during the school year. If you're interested, please email helloworldcodecamp@gmail.com. The program was made possible by a wonderful group of volunteers, a generous grant from the National Center for Women and Information Technology, and assistance and space (and web hosting!) from The TEK Center.
