Dog Rescues in Oregon


Written By E.O.

There are dogs in this world who need your help, for example The Oregon Dog Rescue has been helping dogs since 2007.

They rescue dogs from shelters in California, who are going to be put to sleep.

So, come rescue a dog who needs your help instead of adopting a dog from a pet store.

It will be your best friend for ever on, be a hero to your community and

invite them to your world

-Nylabones of all sizes
-Trifexis or Simparica of all sizes
-Amazon gift cards
-Printer Paper
-New or gently used dog beds of all sizes
-Pill Pockets
-String Cheese
-Paper towels
-bleach
-thin 4 foot leashes
-Costco kirkland puppy food
-HE liquid laundry detergent
-Nature's Domain Salmon Dog Food from Costco

These dogs have been abused or have never been with a real family, in a real home forever.

They need your help, help them by donating to the "Oregon Dog Rescue"
