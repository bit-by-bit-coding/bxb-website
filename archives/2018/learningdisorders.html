Learning Disabilities

There are many different types of learning disabilities. To name a few: attention deficit hyperactivity disorder, dyslexia, dyscalculia, dysgraphia, and various processing deficits. All can hinder a child's ability to focus, work, or function properly in a classroom setting. Here, you can know how they come about, how they affect a child's mind, and how to help them.
