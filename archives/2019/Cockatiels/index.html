Feeding
Feeding your Cockatiel is very important. You should feed them about twice a day. The main Cockatiel diet is mostly based on seeds and pellets, but about once a week you can give them fruit or vegetables. About once a month, you can give them a treat like a honey stick! My favorite are the treats, I love my honey sticks!!

Bathing
Bathing your Cockatiel keep them clean, otherwise their feathers will get dusty and dirty. Ever once and a while you can fill a bathtub or sink with about 1/2 inches to 2 inches of warm water and let your bird splash around! I love warm baths, they make me nice and toasty before bedtime!
