Hello World Websites!

These websites were made by girls going into 6th through 9th grade in just one week as part of a summer program called Hello World! They are all marvellous and definitely worth a look. The program will run again in the the summer of 2020, and a less formal web-development program may be offered during the school year. If you're interested, please email {{site.contact.email}}. The program was made possible by a wonderful group of volunteers, a generous grant from Thermo Fischer Scientific, and assistance and space (and web hosting!) from The TEK Center.
