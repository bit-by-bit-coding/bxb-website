// Hello World

Hello World is a one-week camp for motivated girls to learn to build their own websites, getting down-and-dirty with real code. Every camper will learn the principles of front-end web development and the heart of CSS, HTML, and JavaScript. Moreover, she’ll get the techniques, resources, and passion to continue tinkering with code for the rest of her life! The program is in its third year and is made possible by a grant from Thermo Fisher Scientific.
